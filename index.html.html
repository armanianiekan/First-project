ABOUT ME



Hello! Welcome to my personal web page. My name is Aniekan Umanah, co-founder of Pitch Higher. Pitchhigher is a new high-tech company and Entrepreneurship service provider. It brilliantly connects the venture capitalist industry. It is designed to stand out as a brand that bonds Business startup owners, Inventors, Entrepreneurs with investment bankers and vice versa.

The venture capitalist industry has 4 major players: Entrepreneurs who need funding; investors who want high returns; Investment bankers who need companies to sell; and Pitchhiger who makes money by creating a market for the other three.

It also establishes itself as a comfortable environment for investors to grow and expand their net worth.
